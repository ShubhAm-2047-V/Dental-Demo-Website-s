FAQs

Common Oral Health Queries

Find answers to the most common dental procedure questions asked by our patients.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend scheduling a routine check-up and professional dental scaling every 6 months to maintain optimal gum health, identify minor cavities early, and preserve enamel surfaces.

Are dental implants permanent?

Yes, dental implants are considered the most permanent solution for missing teeth. The titanium post fuses with your jawbone through osseointegration, acting as a lifetime root for custom-crafted crowns.

What is cosmetic smile designing?

Smile designing is a tailored cosmetic process where our specialists analyze your facial proportions, lip lines, and tooth geometry to construct a fully customized aesthetic makeover plan using veneers, composites, and whitening.

Is teeth whitening safe for enamel?

Absolutely. Our laser teeth whitening treatments are fully supervised by aesthetic clinicians and utilize advanced neutral pH hydrogen gels which break down organic stain molecules without damaging the hard enamel structure.

Do you offer emergency dental services?

Yes, DentalLux offers immediate care for dental emergencies including severe toothaches, chipped crowns, swelling, and facial trauma. Please call our hotline directly to schedule an urgent slot.
